3D Chess on an 8×8×8 Cube

This isn't chess rendered on a pretty 3D board. It's chess reinvented for three dimensions. The board is an 8×8×8 cube—512 cells across eight stacked layers. Every piece has reworked movement rules that extend into the vertical axis, creating a game with dramatically more possibilities than standard chess.

How 3D Movement Works

All pieces begin on the center layer in a standard chess formation. From there, the vertical dimension opens up. Rooks slide along 14 directions: the six axis-aligned lines plus eight planar diagonals that move through two axes while keeping the third fixed. Bishops move along 12 different directions: within-layer diagonals and 3D space diagonals that cut through multiple planes at once. Queens combine everything—all 26 possible directions. Knights make L-shaped jumps across the xy, xz, and yz planes, giving them up to 24 target squares from a central position. Pawns can advance forward, move up or down a layer, or take a diagonal staircase path—and they capture diagonally forward across layers. There's no castling or en passant.

See Everything, Control Everything

Navigating a 512-cell board sounds daunting, but the interface is designed to keep things readable. Drag to rotate the cube and view the position from any angle. Scroll or pinch to zoom in on a specific layer or zoom out for the full picture. Layer toggles let you show or hide individual layers so you can focus on the action. The frosting slider adjusts layer transparency, letting you see through the board to spot threats from above or below. Threat highlighting and move previews show you exactly where each piece can go.

Multiple Ways to Play

Choose from three game modes: Classic for a clean 8×8×8 experience, Barricade which adds extra pawns on adjacent layers for a tighter, more tactical opening, and Pawn Wall for a different strategic challenge. Play against the AI at three difficulty levels, or go online with multiplayer—create a room, share an invite code, or jump into Quick Play matchmaking. The game is completely free and runs instantly in your browser. No downloads, no plugins, no accounts. Desktop and mobile.
